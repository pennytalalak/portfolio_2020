About Me

Penny Talalak

User Experience | User Interface Designer | React Developer

Penny is a graduate Bachelor of Computational Design at the University of New South Wales specialising in Interaction Design. Her main interest has always been understanding people's behaviour through psychology and user research in order to deliver a solution with a purpose empathetically.

I believe anything can be automated and simplified to a beautiful & user-friendly application.

Penny was introduced to data-driven design from working at the NSW Department of Education to design an Information Asset Management platform. In 2018, Penny worked at Arup, an Engineering consulting company predominantly designing for B2B and enterprise SASS products. Her data visualisation and analytical skills allow her to understand the database in order to bring the scale of complexity to a seemingly simple application for Engineers.

Currently, Penny is a UX/UI Designer at InfoTrack, an online title searching company for lawyers and conveyancers. Her recent work is designing for a Blockchain application.
