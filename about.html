About Me

Penny Talalak

User Experience | User Interface Designer | React Developer


Skills

  • UX/UI: Sketch, InVision Studio, Figma, User Interview, Mapping
  • Development: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React.js
  • Graphic: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effect, Premier Pro

Penny has over 3 years of experience in human-centred design, with her specialities in User Experience and User Interface design both in Agile environment and Innovative space. Her data visualisation skills have allowed her to bring the scale of complexity in the dataset to a seemingly simple application within the B2B SaaS product where her work has been mainly focused on.

I believe anything can be automated and simplified to a beautiful & user-friendly application.

As a full-stack designer, Penny has been able to apply her skills in the Government sector, previously working with NSW Department of Education, NSW Police Force and Engineering sector, Architecture and Legal industry.

In a multi-disciplinary team, Penny has extensive experience working with Business Analysts (BAs), Project Lead and Developers in an Agile environment. As such, Penny has always delivered in a timely manner and ensures high quality work is presented throughout each sprint. In addition, Penny is SCRUM Master Certified which allows her to understand Agile framework in depth.

Penny's latest project was being part of the end-to-end product development with the NSW Police Force. This includes hosting a workshop, conducting user interview, user testing and wireframing/prototyping with Agile team.

Entrepreneurship

After graudating from Bachelor of Computational Design with Distinction and three times Dean’s List Award, Penny is also an Innovation advocate with experience in mentoring for the UNSW Pre-Accelerator New Wave Program for aspiring female entrepreneur, pitched businesses to the public and a speaker at the Sydney Designer Meetup Group and Career Events. In 2021, Penny joined the Innovation team at a Legal Tech called InfoTrack where she designed and successfully launched 6 working products within 6 months to solve complex legal solution.

Within the same year, Penny also established her e-commerce business called Pocketi with a mission to collect happy moments by turning your favourites into wearables! Within 6 months of launching, she has gained more than 6000 organic Instagram followers, sold more than 200 products, expanded to the United States, Canada and UK, and collaborated with 6 high profile influencers with the business becoming profitable within 6 months.
