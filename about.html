About Me

Penny Talalak

User Experience | User Interface Designer | React Developer


Skills

  • UX/UI: Sketch, InVision Studio, Figma, User Interview
  • Development: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React.js
  • Graphic: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effect, Premier Pro

Penny is a graduate from Bachelor of Computational Design (with Distinction) at the University of New South Wales specialising in User Experience Design. Her main interest has always been understanding people's behaviour through psychology and user research in order to deliver a solution with a purpose empathetically.

I believe anything can be automated and simplified to a beautiful & user-friendly application.

Penny was introduced to data-driven design from working at the NSW Department of Education to design an Information Asset Management platform. In 2018, Penny worked at Arup, an Engineering consulting company predominantly designing for B2B and enterprise SASS products. Her data visualisation and analytical skills allow her to understand the data set in order to bring the scale of complexity to a seemingly simple application for Engineers.

Currently, Penny is a UX/UI Designer at InfoTrack, a LegalTech company focusing on bringing a better experience for lawyers. As part of the Innovation team, the team works tightly together to bring new and exciting ideas & technology to the business.

Entrepreneurship

Penny offers a pro bono work for ambitious students/graduates who are working to establish a start-up or a non-profit organisation. Work mainly includes web design and app design to bring their ideas to life. Penpitch Project started from the desire to combine illustration skills and coding skills together.

Penny targets student’s start-up and/or student’s non-profit organisation that are in need of design help in order to executing their idea. As a pro bono side project, Penny sees success when her design has been contributed to a great cause and business.
